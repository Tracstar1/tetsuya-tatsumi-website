Nid Drasil

Aliases: Draconic Akashic, The Dragon Alchemist

Age: 15 | Gender: Male

Appearance:
Light brown skin, light blue eyes, short spikey black hair with a ponytail, diamond symbols on his hands, long black-purple leather jacket, white dress shirt with blue tie, black pants, and black shoes.

Favorite Food: Chicken Wings

Personality: Intelligent, sarcastic, and indifferent to most — but fiercely loyal to Eter.

Abilities — Draconic Alchemy: Roar, Gekirin, Scale Spirits

Bio: Nid fights for the Hidden Library, defending humanity from Elementals. His mastery of Draconic Alchemy makes him one of the youngest yet most formidable Akashic warriors alive.

Eter

Aliases: Eter the Dracomunculus, Eter of the Elementals

Age: 7 | Gender: Male

Appearance:
White skin, silver-blue eyes, silver-white hair, dragon tail, monk robes, and childlike energy.

Favorite Food: Everything edible

Personality: Playful and kind-hearted, but fiercely protective of Nid and dangerous when provoked.

Abilities — Draconic Alchemy: Roar, Aether Eater

Bio: A mysterious dragon child bound to Nid Drasil. His powers are immense, but his innocence often masks the danger within.
